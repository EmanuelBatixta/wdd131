São José City Park

The Roberto Burle Marx Municipal Park, better known as "Parque da Cidade", located in São José dos Campos - SP, occupies an area of ​​around one million square meters that was part of the old Parahyba Weaving Farm.This is a good place to do a picnic and rest with th family

Vicentina Aranha Park

Vicentina Aranha Park is located in the city of São José dos Campos, it is a historical heritage site of the city listed by COMPHAC (Municipal Council for the Preservation of Historical, Artistic, Landscape and Cultural Heritage of the Municipality of São José dos Campos) and Condephaat (Defense Council of Historical, Archaeological, Artistic and Tourist Heritage). Is a good place to cultural activity and physical.
